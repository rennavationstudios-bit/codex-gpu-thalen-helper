# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone. Be constructive, assume good faith, focus criticism on technical work, respect privacy, and do not publish another person's credentials or private data.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized conduct, doxxing, deliberate disruption, or abuse of vulnerability reports.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior threatens people or the project. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Enforcement decisions will prioritize safety, context, and proportionality.
